Gohan hates fighting because it's never been anything but traumatizing for him. Although, he doesn't like to admit it, he loves Martial Arts and the adventure it brings.
He enjoys his superhuman powers, especially when utilizing them as a superhero. He then went on to create the persona, The Great Saiyanman.
He desires to surpass his father, but wishes to do so on his own terms and methods. Rather than depend on any Super Saiyan transformations, he focuses on empowering his natural and unamplified power.
